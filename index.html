🚀 Space Exploration

Journey Through the History of Space Missions

1957

Sputnik 1 launched by USSR — first artificial satellite in space.

1969

Apollo 11 mission lands humans on the Moon.

1998

International Space Station (ISS) construction begins.

2012

Curiosity Rover lands on Mars to explore the planet.

2021

Perseverance Rover lands on Mars and searches for ancient life.

🔭 Future Space Missions

2030

First human mission to Mars.

2040

Permanent human base on the Moon.

2050

Commercial space tourism becomes common.
